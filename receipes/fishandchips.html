Fish and Chips Recipe

Ingredients

  • Salt
  • flour
  • 1/2 tbsp - flour
  • potato or corn starch
  • 1/2 tbsp - garlic powder
  • 1/2 tbsp - onion powder
  • black pepper
  • baking powder
  • 1/2 tbsp - salt
  • 170ml of carbo water or beer
  • ice cube to keep it cold

Steps

  1. pat the cod dry
  2. season it with salt and black pepper
  3. cote it in flour
  4. put potato or corn starch, garlic powder, onion powder, black pepper, baking powder, salt, carbohydrate water or beer, ice cube in a bowl
  5. fry it at 350F for 5 to 8 min
