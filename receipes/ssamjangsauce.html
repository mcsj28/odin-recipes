Ssamjang Sauce Receipe

Description

A korean sauce you eat with lettuce and meat

Ingredients

  • soybean paste
  • pepper paste
  • green onion
  • Minced garlic
  • onion
  • sugar or honey
  • sesame oil
  • seseame seeds

Steps

  1. 1/2 cup of Soybean paste
  2. 1 tbsp of pepper paste
  3. green onion
  4. onion
  5. 2 tbsp of onion
  6. 2 tbsp of sesame oil
  7. sesame seeds
  8. mix it all up in a bowl
