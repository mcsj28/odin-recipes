Tofu Kimchi Receipe

Description

A traditional korean dish consisting of boiled tofu and fried kimchi

Ingredients

  • oil
  • kimchi
  • garlic
  • sugar
  • sesame oil
  • pepper flakes
  • sesame seed
  • boil water

Steps

  1. put oil in a pan
  2. 400g of Kimchi
  3. 1/2 tbsp of Garlic
  4. 1/2 tbsp of Sugar
  5. 1 tbsp of sesame oil
  6. 1 tbsp of pepper flakes
  7. 1/2 of Sesame seed
  8. put tofu in a boiling water for 5 min
