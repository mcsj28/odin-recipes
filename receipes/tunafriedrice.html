Tuna Fried Rice

one can of tuna, one cup of kimchi, one cup of green onion, one cup of diced onions, rice, one cup of gochujang, one cup of gochu garu

Ingredients

  • tune - one cup
  • kimchi - one cup
  • green onion - one cup
  • diced onions - one cup
  • oil
  • Rice - two cups
  • gochujang - one cup
  • gochu garu - one cup

Steps

  1. dice kimchi in to small pieces
  2. cut green onions and onions in to small pieces
  3. splash oil
  4. put kimchi, green onions, onions, and tuna
  5. put gochugaru
  6. put rice
  7. put gochujang
  8. fry an egg and put it on top of the finished tuna fried rice
